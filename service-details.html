Traditional Oil Portrait

The Timeless Beauty of Oil

My traditional oil portraits are created using the finest artist-grade materials. Each piece begins with a custom-toned canvas and layers of carefully applied oil paint to create depth, luminosity, and life.

This medium is perfect for those looking for a centerpiece that will become a family heirloom. The rich texture and vibrant colors of oil paint offer a presence that no other medium can match.

The Process

1
Consultation & Photo Review

We discuss your vision, background preferences, and select the perfect reference photo.

2
Sketch & Underpainting

I map out the composition and tonal values to ensure a perfect likeness.

3
Color Layers

Building up the portrait through multiple sittings, focusing on the fine details of fur and expression.

4
Drying & Shipping

Once finished, the painting needs time to dry before being varnished and safely packed for shipping.

Photo Requirements

  • High resolution (Smartphone photos are usually fine)
  • Natural lighting (Outdoor or near a window)
  • Eye level with your pet
  • No blurry or dark photos
